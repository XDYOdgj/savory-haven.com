  This tiny, portable air pump is perfect for camping or outdoor activities. The mini size (2*1.7*1.7 inch) and lightweight (3.1 oz) make it easy to carry. This compact air pump is ideal for adventurers and campers who need a versatile, portable solution for inflation and lighting during outdoor activities.
